curiousVitae


We want to introduce you to the new social media platform ‘curiousV(itae)’, a simple and smart way to timeline, track and share your interest journey with your very own community to inspire others as well as getting inspired by others. It is a modern way of creating and presenting a CV, your ‘Interests CV’, in the middle of a social media-based surrounding!

Vision


Our vision is to enrich people by self-realization for their genuine interests and curiosity and link them together. Moreover, treating interests and hobbies in a more sustainable and valuable way is supposed to affect the future career choices of young people, e.g., children and teenagers, and create a greater joy of life and a stronger community within human lives..

Mission


Our mission is to create the perfect tool for building a ‘journey line’ for your (new) hobby. It is supposed to be the perfect inspiration and support for others and the perfect motivation for yourself - to visualize different milestones of your learning progress and success through ‘summons to create’.

Core Value

The core value of our product consists out of four functionalities:

  1. Share different learning content of others within your ‘summons to create’, e.g., YouTube videos, online courses, user posts, etc

  2. Share your content within your ‘summons to create’, e.g., self-recorded videos, pictures or audio files, self-authored instructions or self-painted/-drawn images, etc.

  3. Set your daily, weekly, or monthly assignments and reminders

  4. Create your interest-based community
